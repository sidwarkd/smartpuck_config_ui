Smart Puck Setup

Let's get your puck connected!

  1. Use the provided cable to plug in your puck. You can connect it to your phone charger or a computer.
  2. Disconnect from your current WiFi and connect to the new WiFi hotspot created by your puck that starts with 'MySmartPuck'. This is done in your settings and the steps will depend on your device. There is no password so once selected your device will connect to the puck automatically.
  3. Once connected to the puck WiFi press Next

I found your puck! Now we just need to tell it how to connect to your local network. Click the Scan button below to search for wireless networks.

Select your network from the list above, enter your password, and click Connect. The color next to each network indicates how strong the signal is. Green is excellent, orange is ok, and red is unlikely to work. To get a better signal move your puck closer to your WiFi source and scan again.

Don't see your network? Move your puck closer to your WiFi source and then click the Scan Again button above.

Your Puck is Configured!

If it hasn't already, it should update with your team's current stats within the next couple of minutes. After that the stats will be refreshed after each game. All you have to do is keep it within WiFi range and it will take care of the rest.

If your puck doesn't update the most common issue is an incorrect password. If that happens, simply reload this page and go through the configuration steps again.
