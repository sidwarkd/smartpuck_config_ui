Smart Puck Setup

Let's get your puck connected!

  1. Plug in your puck. You can connect it to a wall charger or a computer.
  2. Disconnect from your current Wi-Fi and connect to the new Wi-Fi hotspot created by your puck. It will appear as MySmartPuck-XXXXXX on your network list.
  3. Once connected press Next

I found your puck! Now we just need to tell it how to connect to your local network. Click the Scan button below to search for wireless networks.

Select your network from the list above, enter your password, and click Connect.

Don't see your network? Move your puck closer to your Wi-Fi source and then click the Scan Again button above.

Your Puck is Configured!

If it hasn't already, it should update with the latest stats within the next couple of minutes. After that the stats will be refreshed after each game. All you have to do is keep it within Wi-Fi range and it will take care of the rest.

If your puck doesn't update it's probably a password issue. If that happens, simply reload this page and go through the configuration steps again.
